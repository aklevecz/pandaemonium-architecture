## Summary

Matheny's *Incunabula* catalog documents a shadow history of dimensional travel via obscure publications, quantum mechanics, and consciousness experiments. Exists in the register of ARG/alternate reality game: cryptic references to the "Egg," trans-dimensional technology, and the "Breakthrough" achieved at an Institute for Chaos Studies. Matheny blurs bibliography, conspiracy, and fiction to create a textual artifact that *performs* the paranoia and wonder of hidden knowledge. The catalog is simultaneously real archive and narrative hoax.

## Key quotes

- *"This catalogue has been put together with a purpose: to alert YOU to a vast cover-up, a conspiracy so deep that no other researcher has yet become aware of it … and so dangerous that the 'winding-sheet' imagery in our title seems quite appropriate; we know of at least two murders so far in connection with this material."* (line 38)

## Why it's in the course

Week 6 covers "D&D to MUDs to MMORPGs, ARGs to dreampolitik; ong's hat; the beast, ilovebees, Year Zero, Synydyne, Cicada 3301, Cicada Mach 2, gamejacking, 'occult memetics'." Matheny's *Ong's Hat* is canonical ARG material; *Incunabula* is its bibliographic extension. Models the artist-as-info-architect constructing elaborate alternate narratives.
