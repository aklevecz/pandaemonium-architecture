Life with the artificial Anasazi
Jared M. Diamond

How can the complex dynamics of human societies — such as population
rise and fall, and movement — be explained? Combining masses of data
with computer modelling is a fresh way forward.

step, the trajectory of the model, and com-
pare it with real history to test the accuracy
of the input information and the model’s
assumptions.

archaeological research by applying this
approach to a historical problem: the rise and
fall of the Anasazi civilization of Native
Americans in the southwestern United States.
